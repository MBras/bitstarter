Ever found yourself not knowing what to do next to bring your project to a good end? This application will help you split and define the tasks of your project into chunks which can be dealt with by a single person or small team within a reasonable timeframe. And thus we've got a manageable project with clear-cut targets and easy to achieve deadlines due to the small size of every chunk.
