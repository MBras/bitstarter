FAQ

How does Chunks work?
By constantly asking if a chunk can be done by one/a few person(s) within a few days, else break it down into smaller chunks.
How is this different from brainstorm and mind mapping tools?
On the surface, the method used closely resembles mindmapping tools. Under the hood, the goal goes well beyond brainstorming and will help you to finish your project.
Why include a task list?
This project started out as a simple list to keep track of tasks, but soon I found out that defining the tasks was a more pressing issue. With clear targets we'll end up with more finished projects, hence the shift to defining the tasks, while still keeping track of them.
Can I "get things done" with this system?
As the task list is roughly based on the ideas presented by David Allen in his book Getting Things Done, this should work out really well.
