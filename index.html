Ever found yourself not knowing what to do next to bring your project to a good end? This application will help you split and define the tasks of your project into chunks which can be dealt with by a single person or small team within a reasonable timeframe. And thus we've got a manageable project with clear-cut targets and easy to achieve deadlines.
Marketing Section 2
How does Chunks work?
By constantly asking if a chunk can be done by one/a few person(s) within a few days, else break it down into smaller chunks.
How is this different from brainstorm/mindmap tools?
On the surface, the method used closely resembles mindmapping tools. Under the hood, the goal goes well beyond brainstorming and will help you achieve a finished project.
